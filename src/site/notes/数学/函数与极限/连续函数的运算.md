- 函数$f[g(x)]$由函数$u=g(x)$与函数$y=f(u)$复合而成，$f[g(x)]$在点$x_0$的某去心邻域内有定义，若$\lim_{x\rightarrow x_0}g(x)=u_0$，$lim_{u\rightarrow u_0}f(u)=A$，而$y=f(u)$在$u=u_0$连续，则$\lim_{x\rightarrow x_0}f[g(x)]=\lim_{u\rightarrow u_0}f(u)=A$
